I am a research scientist in AI and Robotics. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Inria in Paris, working with Dr. Shizhe Chen and Dr. Justin Carpentier on Embodied Perception and Real-to-Sim approaches. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from LAAS-CNRS, advised by Dr. Rachid Alami and Dr. Thierry Simeon. My doctoral research focused on developing deep learning methods for multi-robot task and motion planning, more specifically, how to allow multiple robot agents to plan, reason spatially, and collaborate to solve complex, long-horizon manipulation tasks. This work led to several publications in conferences such as ICLR, ICRA, IROS and ECAI.
